Breakfast

Caprese Omelette

This Caprese omelette is a classic Italian breakfast that's easy to make and absolutely delicious. The fresh tomatoes, basil, and mozzarella cheese give this omelette a burst of flavor..

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, diced
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Lunch

Panzanella Salad

This Panzanella salad is a classic Italian dish that's perfect for a light lunch. The bread, tomatoes, and basil give this salad a fresh and satisfying flavor.

  • 2 cups stale Italian bread, cubed
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Dinner

Spaghetti Carbonara

This Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish that's quick and easy to make. The creamy sauce, crispy bacon, and Parmesan cheese make this dish a family favorite.

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Appetizers

Bruschetta

Ah bruschetta, one of the best ways to enjoy the bounty of summer.

  • 4 slices Italian bread, toasted
  • 2 cloves garlic, halved
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
